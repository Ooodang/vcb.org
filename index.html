About the Valley Concert Band

Welcome! The Valley Concert Band, Inc. is a non-profit benefit corporation based right here in Stockton, CA. We are a passionate group of community musicians dedicated to sharing our love of music.

Our Mission

Our primary goal is to raise funds to help give private music lessons to promising students in grades 7-12 in our public schools. We believe every talented student deserves the chance to grow their skills, regardless of their financial situation.
